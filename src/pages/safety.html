How to Report Safely

üìû Immediate Danger

If you are in immediate danger, call the police (112) or emergency services (912) immediately.

Reporting Process

1. Document Everything

Keep records of incidents, including dates, times, locations, and witnesses.

2. Seek Support

Contact a trusted friend, counselor, or our support hotline before reporting.

3. Choose Your Reporting Method

Report to police, school authorities, or trusted organizations based on your situation.

4. Follow Up

Stay informed about the progress and continue seeking support throughout the process.
