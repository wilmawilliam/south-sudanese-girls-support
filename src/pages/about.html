Our Story

EmpowerHer was founded in 2020 by a group of South Sudanese women who had experienced the challenges of pursuing education while displaced. Having navigated similar journeys themselves, they recognized the unique obstacles faced by young South Sudanese girls studying in Rwanda.

What started as informal mentorship sessions has grown into a comprehensive support network that addresses not just academic needs, but also safety, mental health, and community connection. We understand that education is not just about textbooks and exams ‚Äì it's about creating an environment where young women can flourish holistically.

Today, we serve over 500 South Sudanese girls across Rwanda, providing them with resources, mentorship, and most importantly, a sense of belonging and hope for their future.
