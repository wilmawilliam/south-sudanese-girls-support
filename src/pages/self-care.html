Mental Health & Stress Management

üåô Managing Stress

  • Practice deep breathing exercises (4-7-8 technique)
  • Take regular breaks from studying
  • Engage in physical activity or walking
  • Listen to calming music or nature sounds
  • Practice mindfulness and meditation
  • Write in a journal to express your feelings

üòä Dealing with Homesickness

  • Stay connected with family through video calls
  • Create a comfort space with familiar items
  • Cook traditional foods when possible
  • Connect with other South Sudanese students
  • Focus on your goals and future plans
  • Seek counseling support when needed

Building Resilience

  • Develop a daily routine and stick to it
  • Set small, achievable goals
  • Practice gratitude daily
  • Build supportive relationships
  • Learn from challenges and setbacks
  • Celebrate your progress and achievements

When to Seek Help

Reach out for professional support if you experience:

  • Persistent sadness or hopelessness
  • Difficulty sleeping or eating
  • Loss of interest in activities
  • Overwhelming anxiety or panic
  • Thoughts of self-harm
